Our Mission

MissionsPerformance.org is a groundbreaking initiative focused on evaluating the diplomatic efforts of states' Permanent Missions to the United Nations. By assessing key metrics such as voting influence, gender parity, co-sponsorship of resolutions, and overall engagement, we aim to highlight areas for improvement in diplomatic practice.
