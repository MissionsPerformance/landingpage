About Us

Evaluating diplomatic excellence to enhance global multilateralism

Our Mission

MissionsPerformance.org is a groundbreaking initiative focused on evaluating the diplomatic efforts of states' Permanent Missions to the United Nations. By assessing key metrics such as voting influence, gender parity, co-sponsorship of resolutions, and overall engagement, we aim to highlight areas for improvement in diplomatic practice.

Our goal is to showcase the work of the Permanent Missions of the 193 member states, motivating them to increase their contribution to global multilateralism and respect for the UN Charter, which will ultimately enhance the efficiency of the United Nations and help create a better world.

What We Do

Performance Evaluation

We conduct comprehensive assessments of how effectively Permanent Missions engage with UN processes, including:

  • Voting patterns and influence in the General Assembly
  • Gender parity in mission leadership and staff
  • Co-sponsorship patterns of resolutions and initiatives
  • Overall engagement in multilateral diplomacy

Data-Driven Insights

Our research provides transparent, objective analysis based on quantifiable metrics that help identify trends, best practices, and areas for improvement in diplomatic engagement.

Promoting Excellence

By highlighting effective diplomatic practices and areas for growth, we encourage all member states to enhance their contributions to international cooperation and peace.

Our Vision

We envision a more effective United Nations where all member states actively contribute to global problem-solving through enhanced diplomatic engagement. By promoting transparency and accountability in diplomatic practice, we aim to strengthen multilateralism and advance the principles of the UN Charter.

Through our work, we hope to inspire a new generation of diplomatic excellence that prioritizes collaboration, inclusivity, and effective global governance.
